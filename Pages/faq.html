FAQ

  • What are CLEI labels?
    • Visit this page to learn more!
  • I can't use your service because my serial numbers are printed on demand.
    • Try to obtain a list of serial numbers that were printed post-production. If you are able to obtain a list of serial numbers, you can give us the listing along with their respective CLEI codes. Most orders are completed within 3 working days, some may be a little longer. If your products are sitting in a staging area you may be able to order the CLEI code labels and have them shipped to the location where the products will be installed. The labels we print will have the serial number printed so that they will be easier to match to the correct product.
  • I didn't receive an order confirmation.
    • If you do not receive a confirmation within 24 hours please call +1-732-925-1649 OR +1-908-234-2534(CLEI) for immediate attention.
  • What is the average turnaround time?
    • Normal turnaround time is within 5 business days for standard label orders using FedEx standard overnight service.
  • Are international customers responsible for import taxes, duties, and shipping fees?
    • Yes.
  • Do custom and 2D labels require a greater timeframe to receive?
    • Yes, please call us for details.
  • I made a mistake on my orderform. What can I do?
    • PLEASE be sure to double-check your order, we are not responsible for errors on the orderform. We do our best to catch mistakes, but you are ultimately responsible for what goes on the bar code label. If you recently placed the order, don't hesitate to call us.
  • What payments do you accept?
    • You may pay us securely with any major credit card through PayPal.
